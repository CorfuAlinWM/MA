Breakfast

Order Catering

Whether you’re adding some sunshine to your meeting or want to skip the cooking and feed the group, our breakfast catering options are guaranteed to brighten the day. From light fare such as our fresh fruit and yogurt parfait, to our hearty Croissant Sandwich Tray, piled high with egg and sausage, our breakfast catering will start your day off right. Don't forget to add our freshly brewed coffee to your order! All breakfast items are accompanied by plates, napkins and utensils.

Croissant Sandwich Tray

Everyone loves a hearty breakfast sandwich! Croissants piled high with egg, cheese & choice of Black Forest ham, sausage or Applewood bacon. Also available as a wrap.

Fresh Fruit Tray

Take a walk on the healthier side! Our Fruit Trays come with an assortment of fresh-cut fruits served with strawberry yogurt dip. Fruit trays are available in two sizes – regular or large. Perfect option on its own or to compliment any breakfast, certain to leave you ready to tackle the day!

Don't forget the drinks!

We cater beverages, too! Grab our famous Tea to quench your catering thirst.

Breakfast Box

Make it their own! Cater every Breakfast Box to your guests’ liking. Our Breakfast Boxes include an egg & cheese on a croissant or wrap with your choice of Black Forest ham, applewood smoked bacon or sausage. Includes a fresh fruit cup & yogurt, and utensils.

Yogurt Bar

Greek yogurt served with seasonal fruits and assorted nuts.

"The Works" Coffee

Coffee is a must! Keep the crowd awake with our freshly brewed coffee. Served with cups, lids, straws & sweeteners. Serves 7-8.

Catering Set Up

We take care of the details from start to finish to ensure your event is perfect. We'll deliver your order and set up to your liking. We include all necessary supplies: utensils, plates, napkins, condiments and heating equipment (when applicable).

Delivery

When we say "Catering Delivered," we mean it. Each of our restaurants offer the convenience of delivery for catering. Sit back, relax, we'll take it from here. *Delivery fees & order minimums vary by location.
