Teacher & Nurses Sweepstakes

  • Nominate a Teacher and/or Nurse for a chance to win a free catering and a reward code for a tea. Limited one nurse nomination and one teacher nomination per guest. If nominating one teacher and one nurse, both must be included in a single submission through the nomination form. Those nominated are automatically entered for a chance to win a free McAlister’s Deli catering up to $200 in value for them and their staff. Five nurses and five teachers will be randomly selected and notified on May 7 following the end of the nomination period. Limit one teacher nomination and/or one nurse nomination per guest.

*Free Tea Reward

  • Tea reward code valid for 14 days after receiving and can be redeemed towards a Sweet Tea, Unsweet Tea, 1/2 Sweet 1/2 Unsweet Tea, or Tea Lemonade. Excludes flavor shots. Must be a McAlister's Rewards member to redeem. Offer cannot be combined with other discounts, promotions, or offers. Limited one per transaction. Redeemable at participating restaurants in-store, online or in-app. Taxes and fees may apply. Online orders subject to $0.35 service fee, convenience fee (if delivery) and sales tax. No cash value. Void if altered, transferred, reproduced, exchanged, sold, purchased, or where restricted by law.

Free Catering

Those nominated are automatically entered for a chance to win a free McAlister’s Deli catering up to $200 in value for them and their staff. Five nurses and five teachers will be randomly selected and notified on May 7 following the end of the nomination period. Location(s) in proximity to winners are responsible for fulfillment of catering orders. Delivery radius may vary my restaurant. If delivery is not available, pick-up may be coordinated instead.
